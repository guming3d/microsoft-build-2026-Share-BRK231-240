## Slide 13

### What if the teacher isn't smart enough?

Distillation has a ceiling. RL doesn't.

Supervised distillation

Student copies the teacher
Bounded by teacher's quality
Great for cost & speed
Won't solve problems the teacher can't

Reinforcement fine-tuning

Student learns from its own rollouts
Reward defines the goal, not the teacher
Can outperform the model that generated the data
Built for tasks where "good" is verifiable

When the model you'd distill from can't solve the task, RL is the only way up.
